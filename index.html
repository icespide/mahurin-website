About Jerrod

A civil servant, father and husband, Jerrod Mahurin is the elected Prosecutor of St. Francois County, Missouri. Unlike most, Jerrod knew before kindergarten what he wanted to be when we grew up. An attorney. His career choice has never waivered and neither has his desire to serve others.
